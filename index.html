Since 1949, BAEHR has set the benchmark for professional foot and hand care cosmeceuticals ‚Äî trusted by clinics, salons and practitioners in over 40 countries worldwide.

The BAEHR Story

Gustav Baer, an entrepreneur from Hamburg, developed a revolutionary concept in 1949 for the production of high-quality cosmetics specifically designed for the treatment of foot conditions. What began as a single product line quickly became the industry standard for professional podiatry care across Germany and, within a decade, throughout Europe.

The brand's philosophy has always been simple: combine advanced German science with the most effective active ingredients ‚Äî not for mass-market appeal, but for genuine, measurable results. Every formulation is developed to meet the rigorous standards demanded by medical practitioners and aesthetic professionals alike.

"Quality that professionals demand and patients can feel ‚Äî that has been our standard from the very first day."

Why Professionals Trust BAEHR

The proof of BAEHR's quality lies in its adoption by the medical community. Unlike cosmetic brands built around aesthetics alone, BAEHR products are formulated to treat, protect and restore skin. This is why they are found in podiatry clinics, dermatology practices and professional beauty academies worldwide.

  • ISO certified production facilities ensuring pharmaceutical-grade quality
  • Formulations backed by clinical research and practitioner feedback across 40+ countries
  • Active ingredient concentrations (Urea 10‚Äì40%; Clotrimazole 1%; Hyaluronic Acid) for visible results
  • Extensive range: cream foams, balms, oils, ampoules, instruments, gift sets and seasonal specials
  • Available in professional salon sizes and home care retail sizes

Seminars & Training

Proxy Beauty Spain organises regular BAEHR professional development seminars across Spain. These sessions provide hands-on training in BAEHR treatment protocols, equipping practitioners with the skills and knowledge to deliver outstanding results for their clients.

  • Practical, hands-on sessions covering the full BAEHR product range
  • Delivered by certified BAEHR trainers at venues across Spain
  • Certificate of completion provided to all attendees
  • Available for individual practitioners and full salon teams

BAEHR in Spain

Proxy Beauty Spain is the exclusive representative of BAEHR in the Spanish market. Our team offers personalised advice on product selection for specific treatment needs, and we host regular seminars for professionals who wish to deepen their expertise in cosmeceutical treatments and podiatry protocols.

We supply hundreds of salons, clinics and individual practitioners across Spain with the full BAEHR product catalogue. Our dedicated professional account service ensures that every customer has access to the products, support and training they need.
